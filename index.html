Our Story

Welcome to Lounge India Chelmsford, where traditional Indian flavors meet contemporary dining elegance. Our passionate chefs bring authentic recipes passed down through generations, using the finest spices and freshest ingredients.

From aromatic curries to tandoor specialties, every dish tells a story of India's rich culinary heritage. We create an atmosphere where food becomes an experience, and every meal is a journey through the diverse flavors of the subcontinent.

Join us for an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the art of Indian cuisine in the heart of Chelmsford.

Featured Dishes

Butter Chicken

Tender chicken in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce with aromatic spices. A classic that never disappoints, served with basmati rice.

Lamb Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice layered with succulent lamb, saffron, and traditional spices. Slow-cooked to perfection in our authentic style.

Tandoori Mixed Grill

A sizzling platter of chicken tikka, lamb seekh kebabs, and prawns, marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in our traditional tandoor.

Paneer Makhani

Fresh cottage cheese in a velvety tomato and cashew sauce, perfect for vegetarians seeking rich, authentic flavors.

Fish Curry

Fresh fish simmered in coconut milk with curry leaves, mustard seeds, and traditional South Indian spices.

Garlic Naan

Freshly baked bread infused with garlic and herbs, perfect for complementing any curry dish.
